TAARU (Targeted Analytics Advisory & Research Unit) is a boutique strategic research and advisory firm founded by two senior professionals who have spent their careers at the heart of institutional research — serving bulge bracket investment banks, private equity houses, and top-tier consulting and corporate strategy teams.

We are not a generalist research firm. We specialise in the specific intelligence workstreams that drive investment and strategic decision-making: market analysis, competitive intelligence, commercial diligence research, opportunity screening, and IC-ready narrative development.

Our model is deliberately senior-led and small. Every engagement receives direct involvement from our founders — ensuring the quality, nuance, and contextual judgement that institutional clients demand.

Nitant Kumar Arora

Co-Founder & Consultant

Nitant Kumar Arora is an Independent Consultant in Strategic Research & Advisory, with 15 years of experience supporting bulge bracket investment banks, consulting and PE organizations, and corporate strategy teams.

He previously worked with Acuity Analytics in positions of increasing responsibility, most recently as a Delivery Manager, and prior to that with EvalueServe as a Business Analyst. He specializes in financial modeling and valuations, advanced secondary research, market intelligence, competitive benchmarking, and transaction-related analysis, with a strong focus on enabling data-driven decision-making across M&A, growth strategy, market entry, and investment evaluation.

Nitant has led and delivered end-to-end strategic research engagements across Financial Services, Energy, TMT, and infrastructure-adjacent sectors, working closely with senior stakeholders. His work regularly supports opportunity screening, commercial diligence, internal investment discussions, and the development of decision-ready materials including financial models, market assessments, competitive analyses, pitch books, and investment memoranda.

He is known for his hands-on approach, structured thinking, and ability to translate fragmented market data into clear strategic insights. Nitant holds an Executive MBA from IMT Ghaziabad and a Bachelor's degree in Commerce from the University of Delhi.
